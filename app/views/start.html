Tree Preservation Orders (TPOs) preserve tress in the local environment for the public to enjoy.

TPOs may be used to protect specific trees, groups of trees or woodlands from:

  • being cut down
  • topping
  • lopping
  • uprooting
  • wilful damage
  • wilful destruction.

Applying takes around 5 minutes.
